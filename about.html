About Barangay Abongan Administrative Services

The Barangay Abongan Administrative Services and Management Information System is designed to enhance the efficiency of local government operations. This system streamlines administrative tasks, citizen records, and barangay services.

Key Features:

  • Resident Management: Maintain a digital record of residents and households.
  • Document Issuance: Automate barangay clearance, permits, and certificates.
  • Incident Reporting: Log and manage incidents within the barangay.
  • Financial Management: Track barangay budget, expenses, and fund allocations.
  • Transparency and Governance: Ensure accountability through digital records and reports.

This system aims to promote transparency, efficiency, and better governance for the residents of Brgy. Abongan.
